TaiKai High Voltage Substation Projects

Tesla Super Factory

Tesla is the world's largest manufacturer of electric vehicles. Taikai has provided all core switchgear for the related power supporting projects of Tesla's (China) Shanghai Super Factory, and has set a record for the fastest power connection by Shanghai Power Grid Corporation. We have received the only thank you letter from Tesla worldwide.
Products supplied by TaiKai: 252KV GIS.

Brazil Beautiful Mount DC Power Transmission

This project is the first UHV DC transmission project that China won the bid overseas, the first work of China's UHV technology "going global", and also a major project of Taikai's product service "the Belt and Road" strategy going global.
Products supplied by TaiKai: 8000kV DHV DC disconnector, 800kV UHV DC earthing switch. 800kV value hall earthing switch, 400kV valve hall earthing switch, 150kV DC disconnector, 150kV DC earthing switch, 150kV valve hall earthing switch.

Beijing Winter Olympics

TaiKai has participated in the key projects of green power transmission, such as "Zhangbei Flexible DC Transmission Project" and "Fengning Pumped Storage Project", and received a thank you letter from China National Grid Corporation in the electricity protection service.
Products supplied by TaiKai: 550kV GIS, 126kV GIS, 110kV power cable and accessories, 35kV GIS, 35kV transformer, Unmanned Aerial Vehicle Intelligent Patrol Vehicle, TK-650 phase change heat storage equipment.

Construction of Greater Guangdong-Hong Kong-Macao

Taikai has provided power transmission and transformation equipment for many cities in the area, and participated in numerous power transmission projects such as the "500kV Suidong Station Jiekou Storage and Expansion Line Project", the "500kV Kebei Power Transmission and Transformation Project of the Southern Power Grid", and the "500kV Grid Optimization Phase I Project of Western Guangdong", providing escort for the power system in the Greater Bay Area of Guangdong, Hong Kong, and Macao.
Products supplied by TaiKai: 500kV GIS, 550kV semi-enclosed GIS, 220kV GIS, 110kV GIS, 110kV Power Transformer, Online monitoring CM3000.
