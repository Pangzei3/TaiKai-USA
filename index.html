TaiKai High Voltage Switchgear Co.

TaiKai Group was founded in 1980 and it is an employee owned operation. In 2022, TaiKai achieved sales revenue of 2.9 billion USD, and was shortlisted among the "Top 500 Chinese Manufacturing Enterprises" for 5 consecutive years. TaiKai Switchgear is a whole owned subsidiary of the TaiKai Group. TaiKai Switchgear rankes No. 1 in the high voltage breaker market position.

TaiKai Switchgear offers a range of dead tank circuit breakers for ratings from 39KV 31.5KA up to 550KV 63KA for the USA market. we guarantee Quality, Economy, and Fast Delivery!

About Us

Located at the base of the culturally rich Mount Tai, Shandong Taikai High Voltage Switch Co., Ltd. stands as a wholly-owned subsidiary of the Taikai Group. Our company covers an area of approximately 500,000 square meters, including a construction area of 250,000 square meters. We boast a workforce of over 3,800 employees, among whom are more than 800 skilled technical professionals.
TaiKai Switchgear has expanded its business of high-voltage equipments including GIS, CB, disconnectors, and transformers, to over 60 countries and regions, including the USA, Canada, India, Mexico, Brazil, and many more.
